About Me

A data enthusiast on a journey to harness the power of insights and innovation. Currently pursuing my second Master's degree in Business Analytics and Project Management at the University of Connecticut, I have a solid academic foundation from my first Master's in Technology Management (IT and Big Data) from the University of Bridgeport, CT, USA. My thesis on optimizing manufacturing processes using data-driven insights gave me hands-on experience in the entire data science lifecycle.
